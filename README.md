# LIMBO - Provably Fair Game

A modern, sleek prediction-based gambling game inspired by Bluff.com. Predict if the next number will be higher or lower!

- Predict if the next number will go **HIGHER** or **LOWER**
- Win and increase your multiplier up to **1,000,000x**
- Cash out anytime to lock in your winnings
- Each correct prediction increases multiplier by 10%
- **FREE $1,000 CREDITS** - Every player starts with $1,000 in free credits!

## 🎯 How to Play

### Step-by-Step:
1. **Get Free Credits** - Start with $1,000 in FREE credits (no real money!)
2. **Select Your Bet** - Click a preset ($10, $50, $100, $500) or enter a custom amount
3. **Click SET** - Confirm your custom bet amount
4. **Click PLAY** - Start a new game round
5. **Make a Prediction** - Click HIGHER ▲ or LOWER ▼
6. **See the Result** - Next number is revealed
7. **Repeat or Cash Out** - Continue predicting or cash out to win

### Winning Rules:
- ✓ **Correct Prediction** = Multiplier increases by 1.1x, keep playing
- ✗ **Wrong Prediction** = Game ends, lose your bet
- 💰 **Cash Out** = Lock in winnings, keep your profit + original bet
